How To Reach

Vagamon,Kerala

By Air:The nearest airport to Vagamon is the Cochin International Airport(COK) which lies at a driving distance of 95.8 KM via MC Road. From there a bus, or a car, or a cab may be taken to reach the city.

By Road:Via road, you can easily reach Vagamon which is at 100 KM from Cochin. Direct buses ply to and from Cochin, Trivandrum, Madurai, Kottayam and Munnar.

By Train:To reach Vagamon, one must take a train to Kottayam Railway Station(KTYM) which is located at a distance of 64 KM. On reaching the Kottayam station, one can take a bus or a cab to reach Vagamon.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Sky Cycling?

Sky Cycling is an adventure activity that involves riding a bicycle on an elevated cable or wire, usually suspended between two points. It provides a unique and thrilling experience as you pedal through the air, enjoying the scenic views from above.

Where is Vagamon located?

Vagamon is a hill station located in the Idukki district of Kerala, India. It is situated about 1,100 meters above sea level and is known for its picturesque landscapes, green meadows, and tranquil surroundings.

How does Sky Cycling work?

Sky Cycling typically involves a specially designed bicycle that is attached to a pulley system connected to a cable. Riders wear safety harnesses and are suspended in the air as they pedal across the cable.

Is Sky Cycling safe?

Sky Cycling is generally considered safe as long as proper safety measures are followed. It is important to wear the provided safety gear, including a harness and helmet, and to follow the instructions given by the trained instructors.

Do I need prior cycling experience to try Sky Cycling?

While prior cycling experience can be helpful, it is not mandatory. Sky Cycling is suitable for both beginners and experienced riders. The activity is designed to be user-friendly and can be enjoyed by people of various fitness levels.

What is the duration of a Sky Cycling session?

The duration of a Sky Cycling session can vary depending on the specific package or tour operator. On average, a session may last between 10 to 30 minutes. 

Are there any age or weight restrictions for Sky Cycling?

Age and weight restrictions may vary depending on the tour operator. In general, participants should be of a certain minimum age (usually around 10 years) and within a specific weight range (typically between 30 kg to 100 kg). 

What should I wear for Sky Cycling?

 It is advisable to wear comfortable clothing that allows for easy movement, such as sportswear or shorts and a t-shirt. Closed-toe shoes are essential for foot protection.

Can I carry personal belongings during Sky Cycling?

It is generally recommended to avoid carrying any personal belongings, such as bags or purses, during Sky Cycling. Most operators provide lockers or secure storage facilities where you can keep your belongings while participating in the activity.

Can I take my own camera or use a smartphone during Sky Cycling?

The use of personal cameras or smartphones during Sky Cycling may not be permitted due to safety reasons. However, many tour operators provide professional photography or videography services, allowing you to purchase pictures or videos of your experience.
